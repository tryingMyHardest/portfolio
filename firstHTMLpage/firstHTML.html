About Alex's Boat Rental

Alex Doran has been supplying rental boats of all shapes and sizes since 2020. Fake Lake offers the perfect location to spend the day on the water with family and friends in your choice of craft! Come down today and make some memories!

Alex's Boat Rentals is also the perfect spot to bring and launch personal crafts! Our docks include a 30ft long cement launching ramp that accomadates trailers of all sizes. Before you stop in, make sure all boating permits are in order and ready to present. If a permit is required, one can be purchased in person or visit Pennsylvania's Fish and Boat Commission website to register!
